WHAT ARE YOU AIMING FOR?

Effective learning is based on business goals and adapted for the user. It provides the right knowledge, at the right time, at the right price.

E-learning

Reaches a broad and geographically dispersed target group. Available whenever and wherever the user wants. It is easy to follow up who has taken the course and to obtain statistics.

Micro-learning

Learning packaged in small format. Can also be a larger course divided into smaller parts. Basically answering 3 questions: What is it? , What does it? and How can I use it? It is easy to grasp, produce, distribute and follow up and is an important tool for personalizing learning.

Gamification

Activates the brain, boosts the energy level and engage! The user competes, collects points, reaches new levels and receive learning in return. Can be used as homework before, during or after a physical training sessions. To arouse interest, in a positive way inform about changes or engage staff who work from home.

Pod

An easy way to learn while doing something else, taking a walk, doing laundry or cleaning. Can be used to provide prior knowledge before physical training sessions. It can also be used as a forum for sharing information and experiences. For team building and creating a we-feeling.

Video

Short videos with information or instructions are an easy and popular way to gain knowledge. Can be produced in many different ways depending on the area of ​​use and budget. With video cameras, mobile phone, PowerPoint, Doodly etc.

PowerPoint presentations

PowerPoint is an excellent tool for presentations - if used properly. Structure, layout and spices are crucial to give the participants good conditions for learning and a positive experience!

Manuals, Instructions & Guides

In paper form or digitally. How to time report, pay invoices or navigate the intranet. Product information, sales arguments, sales guides, design and visualization of processes.
